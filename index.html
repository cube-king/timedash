Timedash

Time tracking made easy

Timedash takes care of time management and tracking for you.

It's designed to be accessible and easy to use to help you stay focused on your work.

With Timedash, you can easily start and stop timers for different tasks, view your time logs, and analyze your productivity.

  • Start and stop timers with ease
  • Set goals and view progress on the dashboard
  • Track your time effortlessly

This project was primarily created as a way to simplify tracking driving time for Driver's Training - customizable goals with a total overall goal is exactly what I needed to keep track of my driving time. However, this would also apply to coding time in a manner similar to Hackatime! Anything that can or should be timed can be tracked with Timedash. As well, you can set comprehensive and dynamic goals (such as 2 hours a day, 15 hours a week, etc) to boost productivity even further!

Made for HackClub's 2025 "Summer of Making" event!
